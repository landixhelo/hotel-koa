About Us

Discover Tranquil Luxury at Hotel Koa: Your Urban Oasis in the Heart of Tirana

Hotel Koa, situated on Rexhep Hamit Demi Street in the lively city of Tirana, invites guests to experience comfort and convenience in a prime location. Whether you prefer the intimacy of a double or twin room or the added space of a triple room, Hotel Koa offers modern accommodations tailored to your needs. With attentive service and a welcoming atmosphere, it's the perfect base for exploring all that Tirana has to offer.
